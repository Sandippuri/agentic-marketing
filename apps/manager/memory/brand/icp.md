# Ideal Customer Profile

> Fill this in during Phase 3 Day 5 with your real ICP data.
> The Strategist and Content sub-agents read this to understand who they are writing for.

## Who they are

- Role: (e.g.) Head of Marketing or Founder at a B2B SaaS company
- Team size: (e.g.) 2–10 person marketing org
- Stage: (e.g.) Series A–B, 50–500 employees

## Their core problem

- (e.g.) Too much content to manage, not enough signal on what actually moves pipeline.

## What they care about most

- (e.g.) Predictable content output without hiring a content team.
- (e.g.) Knowing which posts drive demo bookings, not just impressions.

## What they are skeptical of

- (e.g.) AI slop that sounds polished but has no point of view.
- (e.g.) Dashboards that show vanity metrics.
