# Brand Visual Guidelines

> Fill this in during Phase 6.5 Day 2 with your real brand assets and design system.
> The Asset sub-agent reads this before every generate_background call.

## Palette

- Primary: (e.g.) #1A1A2E (deep navy)
- Accent: (e.g.) #E94560 (coral red)
- Background: (e.g.) #FFFFFF or #0F0F0F (light / dark)
- Text on dark: #F5F5F5
- Text on light: #1A1A2E

## Typography

- Headline: (e.g.) Inter Bold, size 48–64px
- Body: (e.g.) Inter Regular, size 18–24px
- Avoid: decorative scripts, condensed fonts, more than 2 typefaces

## Banned looks

- Gradients with more than 2 stops
- Drop shadows on text
- Stock-photo-style imagery
- More than 2 brand colors in a single asset

## Aspect ratios by channel

- LinkedIn post: 1.91:1 (1200×628)
- X (Twitter) post: 16:9 (1600×900)
- OG image: 1200×630
- Email header: 600×200
- Poster (standalone): 1:1 (1080×1080)
